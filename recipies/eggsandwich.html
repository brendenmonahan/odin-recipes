Breakfast Sandwich

This was one of the first hot meals I learned to cook. Try different meats such as fried bologna or sausage

Ingredients:

  • 2 pieces of sliced bread
  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice of American cheese
  • 2 slices of cooked bacon

Directions:

  1. Fry bacon in a frying pan. After cooked remove and place on a paper towell
  2. Fry the egg in the bacon greese. Your preference if you want to break the yolk or not
  3. Toast 2 slices of bread
  4. Place slice of cheese on your egg while its still in the pan
  5. Break bacon up and place on top of cheese
  6. Now place your egg, cheese bacon creation on your slice of bread

Bon Appetite!
