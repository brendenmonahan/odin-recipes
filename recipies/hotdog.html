Hot Dog

Hot dogs are a staple at any BBQ. Or you can cook one up in a hurry if you're home in your kitchen

Ingredients:

  • 1 hot dog
  • 1 hot dog bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Sweet Relish

Steps:

  1. Cook hot dog in a fying pan. Use a little butter to get the sizzle going
  2. Toast bun in a toaster oven
  3. Cook hot dog to desired liking
  4. Insert hot dog into bun
  5. Apply ketchup, mustard & relish

Bon Appetite!
