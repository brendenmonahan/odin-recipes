Lasagna

This delicious recipie serves 12 and should be prepared in a 9" x 13" oven safe dish or pan. Prep time: 20 min. Cook time: 45 min.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box lasagna noodles
  • 1 jar tomato sauce
  • 1 package shredded mozzeralla
  • 1 lb hamberger meat
  • 1 container of ricotta cheese

Directions:

  1. In a large pot. Boil pasta. Rincse with cold water. Drain.
  2. In a large sauce pan cook hamburger meat and pour in tomato sauce.
  3. Assembly in 9 x 13" pan in the following order:
    • sauce
    • pasta
    • ricotta cheese
    • sauce
    • mozzeralla cheese
    • repeat until baking dish is filled
  4. preheat oven to 375 degree F
  5. bake for 45 minutes
  6. let cool for 15 minutes prior to serving
